About Us

Myers Group Limited provides consulting, training, and insurance advisory services, leveraging a team of experienced professionals in various fields, including human resources, finance, safety, and legal expertise. The company is dedicated to building long-term partnerships with clients through reliable, high-quality, and targeted solutions. By combining technical and functional knowledge, Myers Group aims to deliver optimal business solutions and maintain close client relationships to help them achieve their objectives. The company's goal is to be the top choice for corporate training, consultancy, and insurance advisory services.

Our Mission

Is to achieve the reputation of a quality, high standard & reliable training, HR consulting and insurance advisory Company in Kenya.

Our Vision

Is to achieve 100% customer satisfaction by delivering quality services at an affordable cost. Our forward vision is to strive to become an entity offering training, HR consulting and insurance advisory, capable of demanding unconditional response from the targeted niche. We also believe that for our scope of services – sky is the limit and we are always ready to take our achievements to the next level. We are growing and would always like to remain on the growing streak.

    • Our JOURNEY

    Myers Group Limited began as a corporate training services provider, offering training in sales management, customer service, leadership, strategy implementation, and risk compliance. We then expanded into insurance advisory by establishing Myers Insurance Agency, providing insurance broking services for group risks, medical, motor insurance, and pensions. Next, we ventured into HR consulting, offering recruitment, job evaluation, and corporate structure alignment services. Today, we continue to strengthen our core areas of focus and remain committed to achieving our vision of growth and excellence.

    2012

    Started the journey as corporate training services provider

    Conducted training to various corporate in the areas of sales and sales management, customer service, leadership training, strategy implementation and monitoring, risk and compliance

    2016

    Stepped into insurance advisory services

    Established Myers Insurance Agency and provided insurance broking services for Group risks, group medical, motor insurance, pensions

    2017

    Started the HR consulting arm in earnest- Recruitment, job evaluation etc

    Focused on providing HR consultancy services and undertook various end to end recruitment for various companies. Also conducted other HR consultancies such as job evaluation, corporate structure alignment etc.

    2018

    Now we are empowering excellence and enabling success

    We continue getting stronger in our key areas of focus and look forward to achieving our vision going forward.
